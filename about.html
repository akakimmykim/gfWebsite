About Me

Hi! I'm currently a rising senior in Boston University, majoring Advertising and double-minoring in English and Innovation & Entrepreneurship.

From across the world, I've grown up in the small, food paradise island of Penang, Malaysia. I attended the only American international school on the island.

With this strange dichotomy of being a Malaysian within an American international setting, I wrote my book, House of Koi as a love letter to my younger, insecure self, in hopes my story will help others find where they belong.
